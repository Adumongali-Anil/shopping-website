Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to enter your shipping and payment information to complete your order.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

What are the shipping costs?

Standard shipping is $4.99, Express shipping is $9.99, and Next Day Delivery is $14.99. Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

How long will delivery take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and Next Day Delivery arrives the next business day.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window for most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging with tags attached.

How do I return an item?

Log into your account, select the order containing the item you wish to return, and follow the return instructions. We'll provide a prepaid shipping label.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

When will I be charged?

Your payment will be processed immediately upon placing your order.

Still have questions?

Our customer service team is here to help!
